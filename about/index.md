- Started out studying Computer Engineering at [Pune Institute of Computer Technology](https://www.pict.edu)
- Began my career analysing data, models and shaping applications of it
- Spent six years working hands-on with various types of data
- Gradually became more interested in how technology is governed, not just how it performs

- This shift led me into AI regulation, ethics, and policy work across jurisdictions
- Wrote and presented a Thesis on [A Sector-Specific Quantitative Analysis of Regulatory Gaps in the U.S. Federal Courts for AI Governance](https://zenodo.org/records/15579528), later included in Data & Policy conference proceedings
